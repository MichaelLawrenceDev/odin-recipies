All Recipies / Mom's French Dip Sandwich

Description

This is one of my family recipies. One of my go to favorites, and a way that I remember her.

Ingredients

  • 2 Pack of French Bread
  • 2ibs of Boars Head Roast Beef
  • 2ib of Munster Cheese
  • Olive Oil
  • 1 Sweet Onion
  • 2 cups of beef broth

Instructions

  1. Dice up sweet onion
  2. Set oven to broil
  3. Start a sauce pan on medium low heat and add beef broth to simmer
  4. Start warming a skillet under medium high heat and add 1 tablespoon of Olive Oil
  5. Add diced sweet onion to skillet and cook until golden brown
  6. Slice french down middle and cut in half to sandwich size
  7. Spoon a little olive oil onto sliced bread and spread (you don't need much)
  8. Cook underside slice of bread in broiler with a cookie sheet until golden brown topside
  9. Repeat above step for topside, but while cooking, layer desired amount of roast beef and munster slices on sandwich
  10. Add sauted onions below cheese while layering, helps keep it together
  11. Insert layered bottom slice into oven and cook until golden brown
  12. Finally, add top bun to sandwich
  13. Slice sandwich in half, and serve with bowl of beef broth
